'An Haunt' is a CYOA novella I have been working on in various forms for about two months as of late June 2016. Each chapter will have a single branching choice consisting of two options. Therefore Chapter 2 will have four choices (though you'll only ever see two on a single readthrough), and so on.

More information about 'An Haunt' will be posted here in the weeks to come.
